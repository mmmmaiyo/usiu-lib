New Additions

July 10, 2017

The Structure of Scientific Revolutions

Thomas S. Kuhn

This seminal work delves into the nature of scientific progress, introducing the concept of paradigm shifts and challenging the conventional understanding of scientific development.

July 10, 2017

The Social Construction of Reality: A Treatise in the Sociology of Knowledge

Peter L. Berger & Thomas Luckmann

This book explores how individuals create and maintain the social order through shared beliefs and meanings, shedding light on the process of socialization and the construction of reality.

July 10, 2017

Season of Migration to the North

Tayeb Salih

Salih's novel explores themes of cultural identity, colonialism, and the impact of Western influence on African societies. It follows the return of Mustafa Sa'eed, an enigmatic figure, from Europe to his village in Sudan.

July 10, 2017

The Second Sex

Simone de Beauvoir

A foundational work in feminist philosophy, de Beauvoir critically analyzes the social and historical construction of gender, addressing issues of existentialism, women's rights, and patriarchy.
